SpeedLap

Professional speed tracking and comprehensive fuel management for iOS

Track Your Speed with Precision

SpeedLap brings professional-grade speed tracking and comprehensive fuel management to your iPhone. Whether you're on a track day, testing your car's performance, managing multiple vehicles, or analyzing fuel consumption patterns, SpeedLap delivers the precision and features you need.

Available on the App Store

Core Features

Everything you need for professional speed tracking, lap timing, and comprehensive fuel management, all in one intuitive iOS app.

Real-time Speed Tracking

Live current speed display with instant updates. Switch between MPH and KM/H with color-coded displays - green for current speed, orange for average.

Professional Lap Timing

Stopwatch-style functionality with unlimited lap sections. Complete history with detailed timing data and session storage.

Dynamic Island Integration

Live Activities show current and average speeds when backgrounded. Compact and expanded views with real-time updates.

Vehicle & Fuel Management

Track fuel consumption, costs, and efficiency across multiple vehicles. Store detailed fuel entries with odometer readings, prices, and efficiency analytics.

Visual Analytics & Export

Built-in charts show efficiency trends and spending patterns. Export everything to CSV, JSON, or direct SQLite database access for power users.

Comprehensive Data Logging

31+ data points per GPS record with accelerometer, gyroscope, and G-force measurements. Import existing data from JSON format.

Safety First

SpeedLap includes a comprehensive safety disclaimer that must be accepted before use. The app is designed for passenger use or when the vehicle is safely stopped. We take safety seriously and require users to acknowledge proper usage guidelines.

Privacy Protected

Your data stays completely under your control. All data is stored locally using SQLite with optional iCloud sync to your personal account only. Use SpeedLap Insights to analyze your exported CSV data locally in your browser.

Requirements

What you need to get started with SpeedLap

iPhone with iOS 16.1 or later for Live Activities
Location permission for GPS tracking
Motion sensor access (automatically granted)
