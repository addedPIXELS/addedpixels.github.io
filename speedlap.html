    a quick look inside

    The app, briefly.

    core features

    Built for people who actually drive.

    ⚡

    Real-time speed tracking

    Live current speed with instant updates. Switch between MPH and km/h. Colour-coded: green current, orange average.

    ◷

    Professional lap timing

    Stopwatch-style functionality with unlimited lap sections. Designed for track days and testing.

    ◖

    Dynamic Island

    Live Activities show current and average speeds when the app is backgrounded. Glance, don't unlock.

    ⛽

    Vehicle & fuel management

    Track fuel consumption, costs, and efficiency across multiple vehicles. Visual analytics and CSV export.

    ▦

    Visual analytics & export

    Built-in charts for efficiency trends and spending patterns. Export to CSV, JSON, or direct SQLite.

    ⚙

    Comprehensive logging

    31+ data points per GPS record. Accelerometer, gyroscope, and G-force measurements at 50Hz.

    safety first

    Designed not to be a distraction

    SpeedLap includes a comprehensive safety disclaimer on first launch. The app is designed for passenger use, or when the vehicle is safely stopped. The Dynamic Island integration is deliberate: you glance, you don't unlock.

    privacy first

    Your data, your device

    All data is stored locally using SQLite, with iCloud sync as an optional opt-in. No analytics by default. Export anything to SpeedLap Insights for browser-based analysis that also doesn't leave your machine.

    requirements

    Anything recent will do.

    • iPhone with iOS 16.1 or later (for Live Activities)
    • Location permission (for GPS)
    • Motion sensor access (granted automatically)
