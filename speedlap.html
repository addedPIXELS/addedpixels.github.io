SpeedLap

Professional speed tracking and lap timing for iOS

Track Your Speed with Precision

SpeedLap brings professional-grade speed tracking to your iPhone. Whether you're on a track day, testing your car's performance, or just want accurate speed data, SpeedLap delivers the precision and features you need.

Core Features

Everything you need for professional speed tracking and lap timing, all in one intuitive iOS app.

Real-time Speed Tracking

Live current speed display with instant updates. Real-time average speed calculation for each section with clear color coding.

Professional Lap Timing

Stopwatch-style functionality with unlimited lap sections. Complete history with detailed timing data and session storage.

Dynamic Island Integration

Live Activities show current and average speeds when backgrounded. Compact and expanded views with real-time updates.

Comprehensive Data Logging

31+ data points per GPS record with accelerometer, gyroscope, and G-force measurements. Professional CSV export included.

Safety First

SpeedLap includes a comprehensive safety disclaimer that must be accepted before use. The app is designed for passenger use or when the vehicle is safely stopped. We take safety seriously and require users to acknowledge proper usage guidelines.

Privacy Protected

Your data stays on your device. SpeedLap doesn't send your location or driving data anywhere - everything is stored locally and only shared when you choose to export it.

Requirements

What you need to get started with SpeedLap

iPhone with iOS 16.1 or later for Live Activities
Location permission for GPS tracking
Motion sensor access (automatically granted)
