Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work offline?

Yes—all your chord sheets are stored locally on your device. No internet connection required for viewing or managing your library.

Do I need an account?

No accounts necessary. Simply download the app and start importing your chord sheets immediately.

Does it support setlists?

Yes—create unlimited setlists and organise songs into sets. Perfect for structuring your performances and keeping your repertoire organised.

What about data collection?

We collect basic usage analytics to improve the app, but never upload your chord sheets or personal content. Your music stays private on your device.

Can I import any PDF format?

ChordLibre works with any PDF file, making it perfect for chord sheets, lead sheets, tablature, and sheet music from any source.

How do I organise large libraries?

Use the alphabetical sidebar, search and filter functions, and create custom setlists. Duplicate detection helps keep your library clean and organised.
