    a decade of evolution

    From a desk in 2016, to an iPad in 2026.

    October 2016

    The original installation.

    During the club's refurbishment we installed a traditional, non-computerised lighting system on a conventional desk. It served the venue well for almost a decade. The world of lighting, in the meantime, had moved on.

    2026

    A complete refit.

    The original system replaced with a modern intelligent rig: 13 fixtures, 289 DMX channels, controlled entirely by our bespoke ShowControl software. Club staff operate the whole thing from an iPad.

    the control system

    The whole rig, on an iPad.

    The entire rig is driven by ShowControl, our bespoke web-based lighting control software. Club staff don't open an app: they open a browser tab and tap the look they want. No app store, no install, no training session.

    Visiting professionals get the bits they want too. Art-Net out, full patch documentation, fixture identify, and no proprietary hardware standing in their way.

    Learn about ShowControl →
    designed for everyone

    Two audiences. One system.

    for club staff & volunteers

    Easy Mode

    The default interface presents large, clearly labelled buttons that anyone behind the bar can use without training. The "show" runs itself.

    • One-tap presets (Houselights, Cabaret, Party Mode)
    • Automatic Live Mode for audio-reactive looks
    • Master dimmer for the whole room
    • No training, no app to install
    for visiting professionals

    Pro tools

    Visiting techs get the open standards they expect. The system runs on Art-Net, alongside LightKey, QLC+, MA Lighting and anything else they bring.

    • Art-Net standard, no lock-in
    • Complete patch documentation
    • Fixture identify on every fixture
    • No proprietary hardware to bypass
