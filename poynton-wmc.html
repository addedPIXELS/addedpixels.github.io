The Story

A decade of evolution

addedPIXELS has a long-standing relationship with Poynton WMC. What started as a conventional lighting install in 2016 became a complete intelligent lighting transformation.

October 2016

The original installation

During the club's refurbishment, we installed a traditional, non-computerised lighting system using a conventional lighting desk. It served the venue well for almost a decade – but the world of lighting had moved on.

2026 – Complete Refit

Intelligent lighting, simple control

A complete refit replacing the original system with a modern intelligent lighting rig – 13 fixtures, 289 DMX channels – controlled entirely by our bespoke ShowControl software. Club staff operate the whole system from an iPad.

What We Installed

The rig

13 fixtures across 289 DMX channels on a single universe, transmitted over Art-Net. We carried out the full physical installation – rigging, DMX cabling, networking, and power distribution.

Moving Head Wash

Stairville SonicPulse Moving Head Wash 1208s

Three wash moving heads with dual-ring RGBW LEDs, 540° pan, 270° tilt, and variable-speed movement. Dynamic colour washes, sweeps, and figure-eight patterns across the dance floor and stage.

Tungsten Sunstrip

Showtec Sunstrip LED Tungsten Blinders

Two 10-segment blinder bars with warm and cool white LEDs per segment. The venue's primary house lights – warm tungsten-style illumination that can be locked on independently, with per-lamp control.

Qty: 2 20ch each WW + CW

LED Bar

Stairville LED Bar 240/8 RGB

Eight 8-segment RGB bars distributed across the venue – five along the stage front and backdrop, one on the DJ side, two on the side walls above the dance floor. Each bar's 8 segments are individually addressable for per-pixel effects.

Qty: 8 24ch each RGB
The Control System

Powered by ShowControl

The entire rig is controlled by ShowControl, our bespoke web-based lighting control software. At Poynton WMC, it runs on a dedicated server accessible via the venue's network.

Club staff operate the system from an iPad – they simply open a web browser and tap the button for the look they want. No app to install, no training required.

Designed for Everyone

Two audiences, one system

Simple enough for any member of staff. Open enough for the touring engineer who wants full control.

For club staff & volunteers

The Easy Mode interface presents large, clearly labelled buttons that anyone can use without training. Live Mode can even run the lights automatically, listening to the audio and switching between scenes as acts come on and off stage.

  • One-tap presets: "Houselights", "Cabaret", "Party Mode"
  • Automatic Live Mode – listens to audio, no intervention needed
  • Master dimmer for global brightness control
  • No training required – learn it in minutes

For visiting professionals

The system runs on Art-Net – the same open standard used by professional desks and software like LightKey, QLC+, and MA Lighting. A visiting engineer can connect to the venue's network and take control with zero friction.

  • Art-Net standard – connect any compatible desk or software
  • Complete patch documentation in the Patch tab
  • Fixture identify – pulse any fixture to confirm which is which
  • No proprietary hardware, no barriers
Key Features

What this venue gets

One-tap operation

Non-technical staff control the entire rig from an iPad. Tap a button, get the look.

17 static scenes

House lights, cabaret, colour washes, and themed palettes – sunset, ocean, forest, arctic, fire, and more.

25 animated programmes

Sound-to-light, disco sweeps, knight rider, police lights, theatre chase, Ghostbusters, Nyan Cat, and more.

Live Mode auto-pilot

Listens to audio and automatically transitions between ambient and performance lighting. Truly hands-off.

Beat detection

Real-time FFT analysis with 3-band frequency splitting – separate responses for kick, snare, and hi-hats.

Moving head calibration

Zone restriction ensures heads can't accidentally blind the audience. Full room, dance floor, stage, or spotlight.

Per-lamp sunstrip control

Individual blinder segments can be toggled on or off, independently of whatever programme is running.

Crossfading

Smooth transitions between any two scenes with configurable fade times – instant to 10 seconds.

Fixture identify

Tap a button to pulse any fixture for visual confirmation during setup – know exactly which fixture is which.
